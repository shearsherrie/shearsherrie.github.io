About Shear Sherrie

Bio

I'm Shear Sherrie and I am here to help you achieve your dream hair and makeup for any occasion! I love, love, love what I do and have been a licensed hair & makeup artist for over a decade!

What I have to offer:

Hair color - from rich all over tones, highlights, bombshell blondes, Victoria Secret inspired balayage, SoCAP & Perfectress Extensions, trendy hair cuts, blowout styles, heated styles, smoothing treatments, deep conditioning love, men's cuts, facial waxing, and of course traditional & airbrush makeup!

I work with Channel 5 news anchors, models, weddings, back in the day - RAM's cheerleaders, film, stage work, fitness & bodybuilding shows, magazine work, pageants, and beautiful every day hair! I strive to stay ahead of the trends in the beauty industry through classes, shows, and experiences so I can offer you the best of the best!

Thanks for checking out my bio!

P.S. Yo hablo espanol y me encantaria resevar tu proximo servicio de pelo y maquillaje!

xo - Shear Sherrie
